Capstone Project

Apply everything from the course into one cohesive campaign

Students will apply everything from the course into one cohesive campaign. You'll choose a business (real or sample), plan, execute, and track a multi-channel digital marketing campaign, then present results in a formal document or slide deck.

This capstone project serves as the culmination of your learning journey, demonstrating your ability to synthesize complex marketing concepts into actionable strategies that drive real business results.

4-Week Project Timeline

Week 1: Research & Planning

Business selection, market research, competitor analysis, and audience profiling. Define objectives and KPIs.

Week 2: Strategy Development

Create multi-channel strategy, budget allocation, content planning, and campaign timeline development.

Week 3: Implementation & Execution

Launch campaigns across selected channels, set up tracking and analytics, begin content creation and distribution.

Week 4: Analysis & Presentation

Analyze results, create comprehensive report, prepare final presentation, and submit capstone project.
